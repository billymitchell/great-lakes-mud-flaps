Great Lakes Mud Flaps started the way many good businesses do: with two brothers, a strong work ethic, and a commitment to doing things right.

Led by the next generation of brothers, Great Lakes Mud Flaps is continuing the family tradition, while modernizing how we operate, communicate, and serve our customers.

We are not trying to reinvent the wheel. We are here to keep this business strong, relevant, and reliable for the next generation of customers, just like it was for the last.
